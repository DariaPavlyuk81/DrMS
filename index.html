About

Dr. Michael Shelling is an exceptional dermatologist whose expertise and compassionate care set him apart in the field. With a deep understanding of various skin conditions, he provides personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient's unique needs. His dedication to patient education ensures that individuals feel empowered to make informed decisions about their skin health. Known for his thoroughness and attention to detail, Dr. Shelling not only treats existing concerns but also emphasizes preventive care, helping patients achieve long-lasting results. His warm demeanor and commitment to excellence make every visit a positive experience, fostering trust and confidence in his care.

Services

Dr. Michael Shelling offers a comprehensive range of dermatological services designed to address the diverse needs of his patients. From treating common skin conditions such as acne, eczema, and psoriasis to performing advanced skin cancer screenings, his expertise ensures accurate diagnosis and effective treatment. In addition to medical care, he provides various cosmetic services, including chemical peels, laser treatments, and injectable fillers, helping patients enhance their natural beauty and achieve radiant skin. Dr. Shelling also prioritizes patient education, guiding individuals on proper skincare routines and preventive measures to maintain healthy skin. With a focus on personalized care and the latest dermatological techniques, his services cater to both medical and aesthetic needs, making him a trusted choice for skin health.
